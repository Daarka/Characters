Personality

A shape-stealing trickster spirit of the desert, Echo has no true name--or even gender. They are a demon evolved from ancient coyotes, a natural-born liar through and through, but whose pack was hunted to extinction. They have instead found kinship with Hazzel and his Shadow as their new found family and partners in crime, with whom they go about their business as a dedicated thorn in the side of local mortals.

Notes

In all variations of their authentic form, their eyes are yellow. The colors between their pelt and skin/hair between forms are, likewise, largely unchanged. Their voice is breathy and rasping, dry and croaking, and they often speak in a lilting cadence that sounds coy and teasing in a vaguely predatory way.

Humanoid Form:

Build • Lanky, thin, and standing at 5'9"; presents androgynous. Their posture is lazy and slouching, but perfectly capable of feats of dexterity in concentrated bursts.
Complexion • Sun-tanned with freckles/sun spots. One irregular, circular scar about the size of quarter on the back of their neck, to the side of their vertebrae (not typically noticeable); no other significant marks.
Hair • Dirty blonde hair of nearly the same color as skintone. Hair is stringy and straight, hangs to approximately their collarbone in length, and is decorated with wooden and bone beads and crow feathers.
Teeth & Claws • Their teeth are mostly humanoid, but have more pronounced canines to the point of being small fangs. They have thick claws in their humanoid form as well, and slender, spidery fingers (though not unnaturally so).
Clothes • Always wearing the shirt, pants, and boots in their reference sheet; they look like fabric but are part of their skin and cannot be removed or altered (sleeves can be rolled up, that's about it). Almost always in the red poncho and with gun and knife holsters strapped on, as well, but those can be removed, so depictions without them are fine.
Blood • Their blood in this form is red as normal, but if it is spilled externally (such as dripping onto the ground), it ultimately dries fully black (if it dries on Echo's body, it maintains the conventional appearance of dried blood as long as it remains touching them).

Coyote Form:

In this form, they are indistinguishable from a normal coyote, with no notable features distinguishing it as otherwise. They are, however, perfectly capable of speech in this form, with their voice sounding identical to how it does in their humanoid form.



True / "Moondevil" / Demon Form:

On all fours, this form is ~6' tall at the shoulder. It resembles a coyote, but has longer limbs and a longer neck, with shoulders that peak up higher than their hips. They can stand on their hind legs, and they have opposable thumbs on forepaws, digits long enough to use like fingers. Their pelt is shaggy, especially on their neck and shoulders, and hangs in almost dreadlock-like tendrils of shed matting.

Other Forms:
While the aforementioned three forms are their authentic body, they will also, situationally, steal the form of some other creature for one reason or another. In most cases, they maintain consistent choices of exact species to imitate whenever they turn into those respective creature types.

Domestic Dog • Tamaskan (wolfdog) - usually wearing a red bandana on neck
Bird • Corvus brachyrhynchos (American crow)
Spider • Habronattus altanus (jumping spider)
Lizard • Coleonyx variegatus (banded gecko)
RELATIONSHIPS
Hazzel
adopted packmate

A very literal partner in crime, Echo has hitched their wagon to Hazzel permanently, who is responsible for giving Echo their name, and also the only reason they're still alive. They consider him family and care for him extremely deeply, they just express it through heckling, biting, defrauding, embezzling, and occasionally identity theft.
